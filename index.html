Abstract

UniBiDex presents a novel approach to universal bimanual dexterous manipulation that enables robots to perform complex tasks requiring coordination between two hands. Our system integrates advanced vision-based control with haptic feedback to achieve precise manipulation capabilities across a wide range of tasks. Through innovative design of the control architecture and extensive experimental validation, UniBiDex demonstrates superior performance in bimanual manipulation scenarios compared to existing approaches.

The system's ability to handle diverse manipulation tasks while maintaining real-time performance makes it a powerful tool for advancing dexterous manipulation and robotic applications in unstructured environments.
